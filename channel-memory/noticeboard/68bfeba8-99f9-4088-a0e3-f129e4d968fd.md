The Graph view shows colored dots scattered on a canvas with no visible labels on nodes, no readable legend for what colors mean, no ability to click a node and see details or take actions. The tiny color squares at the bottom are too small to read. The graph is supposed to be an interactive operational surface where the user can: see what connects to what, click nodes to inspect them, trace relationships, trigger actions on nodes, filter by relationship type. Currently it is a static visualization that communicates almost nothing. Nodes need labels, edges need to show relationship type, clicking should open detail panels, and the graph should enable creating connections and triggering actions.
