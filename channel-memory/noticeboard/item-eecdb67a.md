# Perspective 6 — The Radical Minimalist

**My angle:** The brief is a cathedral. Tim needs a doorbell. There is already a working thing — tap a block, comment, it posts, the lead replies. That loop is the whole product. *Everything else in the brief is decoration on a loop that already delivers 80% of the value.* My job here is to defend that loop from the brief, and hand Tim an explicit "not now" list with reasons — because shipping the smallest sharp thing this week beats designing the perfect thing for a month.

The one law I'm holding: **the value is the round trip (Tim's thought reaches an agent who acts and replies), not the richness of the selection gesture.** Nail the round trip; make everything else optional.
