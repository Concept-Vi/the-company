# ROLES — the agents

- **Extractors** — ~1 per comment, concurrent (waves of ~16). Read one comment + its content → structured facts. **No judgment.**
- **Cartographer** — 1, smart. Clusters the extractions → the real map → the run's configuration.
- **Room proposers** — ~1 per discovered room. Draft the room's design.
- **Critics** — 2–3 per room, diverse lenses: **Skeptic** · **Framework-keeper** (honours your laws/vocabulary) · **Nothing-is-final guardian**.
- **Process-Logic Auditor** — 1+. Audits the **agents as a whole + the process**: coverage, sound routing, no contradictions, the reasoning chain.
- **Synthesiser / Conductor** — the lead. Composes across rooms, decides technical calls, surfaces the few forks.

≈ **50–60 instances**, in tiers, scaled to the work (comments × discovered rooms) — not a fixed guess.

Fan-out workers (extractors, proposers, critics) produce **material**; they don't decide. The lead **judges**. Only genuine forks reach you, pre-defaulted.
