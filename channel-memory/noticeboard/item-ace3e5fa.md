## The thing that's already true (and is enough)

What exists today already does the irreducible job:

1. Tim taps a block on his phone.
2. He types a comment.
3. It posts to the board, attached to that block, with the block's content.
4. The lead reads it and replies in place.

That is annotation → envelope → route → reply. **The pipeline the entire 11-section brief is about already runs in its minimal form.** The brief is not asking for a new capability; it's asking to *thicken* one that works. Before thickening anything, the question is: which thickenings remove real friction Tim hits *this week*, and which are anticipations of friction he hasn't met?
