# REQUIREMENTS — the laws the machine obeys

1. Every comment carried **with its content** — never isolated.
2. **Distributed** — never one AI in one turn.
3. **Extract vs judge** — fan-out workers extract/propose; they don't decide; the lead judges.
4. **Grounded in the originals + context + memories first.**
5. **Diverge, not converge** — preserve tensions, never flatten.
6. **Nothing is final** — candidates held open; a foothold, not an answer.
7. **Re-surface each comment's outcome at its anchor.**
8. Runs **on the channel, through the MCP tools** — agents are channel members.
9. **Coverage** — every comment accounted for (the auditor enforces).
10. **No drift** — every change through the change-pipeline (understand→locate→implement→validate→document→deploy-live).
11. **Translate to meaning** — no jargon; your confusions answered.
12. **Discovered, not templated** — rooms + outcomes emerge from the recon.
13. **Defaults on every fork** — nothing blocks you.
14. **Render for cognition** — navigable, scannable; your recognition is the compute.
15. **You never drive** — the collective runs it; a stall is the lead's failure.
