## One-line stance

His gesture is the right *idea* (modulate scale in one continuous hold, batch then submit) wired to two *wrong specifics* (vertical free-drag, and long-press-as-primary that collides with iOS natives) — keep the idea, retune to **tap-for-default / long-press-to-escalate, stepped detents with live outline + best-effort haptics, an arc-not-circle V in the configurable thumb zone** — and treat iOS-Safari's gesture conflicts and the no-web-haptics limit as first-class, device-verified risks, never assumed-green.
