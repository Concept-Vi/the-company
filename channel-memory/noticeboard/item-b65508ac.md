## One-line for synthesis
The architecture is cleaner than Google Docs/Figma/Hypothesis — but it inherits their three scars unless designed against them: **anchor by redundant text-quote + content-hash (re-resolved at read, flagged-loud when stale), not by stored position** (Figma/Docs orphaning); **split intent into comment/question vs. change-request-with-accept-able-diff** (Docs/GitHub suggestions); and **persist the batch draft-queue durably with a send-now escape hatch** (GitHub's review batch, minus its friction) — all on a tap-to-select / long-press-for-power gesture that wins the iOS scroll/callout war only on real-device verification.
