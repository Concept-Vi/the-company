### ❌ "Annotation system as a reusable company component," graph-feeding, session-as-first-class-object (BD-K intent-level)
These are architecture-beautification goals — make the loop a reusable primitive, feed annotations into the knowledge graph, formalize a "review session." All real, all the system's stated direction, **all premature.** You earn the right to generalize a component *after* it works in one place and you've felt where it's actually reused. Generalizing first is the classic trap that turns a one-week ship into a three-month framework. Build the specific phone-review loop; extract the primitive later, from evidence, if a second surface ever wants it.
