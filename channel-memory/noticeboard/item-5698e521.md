# Perspective 3 — the Graph / Data-Model Architect

**My angle:** I don't care about the gesture or the overlay. I care about the *shape of the thing that gets stored*. Everything Tim wants — multi-scale selection, "everything under a heading," editable-location, replies, freshness — is a single question: **is the document an addressable typed TREE of nodes, and is the annotation a typed observation on a node?** It is not, yet. The atoms exist; the molecule that this brief needs (a parsed hierarchical tree + a stable node-identity that survives re-parse) is the one thing genuinely missing, and it's the thing the brief most under-specifies.

I disagree with the brief on two points of fact, and I'll mark them as I go.
