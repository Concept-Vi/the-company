### 9. Community plugins (broad) — MOSTLY NONE; case-by-case.
The ecosystem is real but most plugins assume human-authored, wikilink-connected vaults. They don't read our typed-address edges and many *write*. Treat as a long-tail to mine opportunistically (e.g. a calendar/timeline plugin could read our `created` field — clean, read-only), never as a category-level adopt.
