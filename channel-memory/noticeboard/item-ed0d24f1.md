### ❌ The real document MODEL / addressable AST (BD-C, prerequisite in BD-K)
"Intelligent" multi-scale selection needs parsing content into a semantic tree (heading owns subtree, list owns items, paragraph owns sentences, every node addressable). This is named as a *hard prerequisite*. **But it's only a prerequisite for the gesture system we just deferred.** The current flat block list is sufficient for tap-a-block-comment. Don't build an AST to power a gesture nobody's confirmed they need. The flat list is the lean substrate; the tree is a maximalist substrate for a maximalist gesture. Cut both together — they stand or fall as a pair.
