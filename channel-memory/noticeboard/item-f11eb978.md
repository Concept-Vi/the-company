### ❌ The radial menu / command center (BD-G)
A radial menu with mode-toggle, draft queue, submit, inbox, supervisor chat, decisions-needing-eyes, settings. **Radial menus are a design-flourish that's hard to do well on a phone and easy to do badly.** Everything it's meant to contain can live as flat, boring, thumb-reachable elements: a submit button (bottom), a replies badge (top bar), a doc switcher (hamburger). Tim asked for "the V symbol that opens stuff" because that's the pattern he's seen — but the *function* is "reach my session controls," and a simple bottom bar or single expanding button delivers that without the radial-geometry rabbit hole. Build flat controls now; consider the V-overlay only if the flat controls feel cluttered.
