# The governing constraint

Build under TIM's principles and architecture — which you learn from Tim directly and from your research — NOT from any plan. The lead deliberately wrote background, problems, and principles only, and NO solution, because:
- A lead-authored plan biases the build (Tim's explicit law: the only harmful thing is writing plans/specifics that influence other agents).
- The lead's knowledge of Tim's full architecture is partial — and that's fine; the gap is filled by Tim + research, not by guessing.
So: research first, understand the architecture with Tim, then build properly through it. Everything on the board is MATERIAL and CONTEXT — never a prescribed answer.
