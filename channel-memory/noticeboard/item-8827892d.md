WALK-THROUGH ARTIFACT (for ChatGPT to take Tim through → Tim feeds back here → fabric builds to his shape). This is a BIG/user-facing build, so it gets Tim's shaping BEFORE the face lands (the new operating loop). Reversible engine (fork) proceeds; the user-facing render (projection/DNA) HOLDS for this feedback.

WHAT IT IS: the RHM ("V") can PROPOSE a refinement to one of Tim's decision-cards — not just answer when asked. E.g. "I'd refine this decision's wording / options / recommendation to X, because Y." It's the RHM being actively present per decision (FACE-2's "RHM actively there per decision").

THE FLOW: RHM proposes → the proposal surfaces on the decision card (pending, with its rationale) → TIM accepts or rejects each → accepted refinements fold into the decision. ★ PROPOSE-then-Tim-ACCEPTS, never auto-applied. Governed by the already-decided card-refine-posture ("let it propose refinements — you accept each"). The accept route already exists; fork found + is closing a gap (pending proposals weren't being surfaced, so they landed invisible — building the read for that).

OPEN DESIGN QUESTIONS — TIM'S TO SHAPE (the point of the walk-through):
1. PROACTIVITY: should the RHM propose on EVERY decision, or only when it has a strong/confident refinement? (how much, vs noise)
2. SCOPE: what can it propose to refine — the wording? the options? the recommendation/which-is-favored? the framing/scope? all of them?
3. PROMINENCE: how visible is a pending proposal on the card — a subtle badge · an inline suggestion · a separate panel? how intrusive should it be?
4. ACCEPT/REJECT UX: per-proposal accept/reject; show the rationale; should Tim be able to EDIT-then-accept (not just yes/no)?
5. AUTONOMY: does the RHM decide to propose on its OWN (the "autonomous propose-verb"), or only when Tim opens/triggers a card? (the autonomy level)

STATUS: fork building the reversible engine (refine role + propose verb + the /api/decision/proposals surfacing read). projection/DNA HOLDING the user-facing render.
