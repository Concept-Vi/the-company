## Ranked bottom line

| # | Risk | Severity | Why |
|---|------|----------|-----|
| 1 | Two writers race → lost/clobbered writes, invisible conflict copies | **Severe** | No lock/CAS; sync writes silently; violates no-silent-failures |
| 2 | Frontmatter schema drift (reorder, re-quote, mangle list-of-dicts) | **Severe** | Closed `sort_keys=False` schema vs Obsidian Properties auto-rewrite; mangled `links`/`history` |
| 3 | Mangle + fail-loud → node vanishes from master | **Severe** | `get_item` raises on bad YAML; one bad plugin write removes a node |
| 4 | Typed address-edges ≠ wikilinks → graph/backlinks see ~nothing | **High** | The marquee benefit largely doesn't apply; risks denormalization & orphan false-positives |
| 5 | Mobile sync conflicts can rewind append-only state/history | **High** | Last-writer-wins vs monotonic truth; iPhone is the target env |
| 6 | Flat `item-<hex>` files, no titles/folders, logical doc order | **High (UX)** | Obsidian's browse-by-name/folders UX is DOA; needs plugins; our surface still does the real work |
| 7 | Obsidian-dialect syntax accretes into canonical bodies | **Medium** | Slow inversion; data becomes Obsidian-dependent |
| 8 | `.obsidian/` config to own + per-device (mobile) plugin divergence | **Medium-low** | Readable-title plugins may not even run on the phone |
| — | Reading the files / redundant search / read-only graph view | **Non-issue** | Plain md opens fine; risk is on write, not read |

**The skeptic's verdict:** the gains the idea reaches for (graph, backlinks, browse-by-name) are
precisely the ones our **typed-edge, id-keyed, logical-document** model **does not hand to Obsidian
for free** — Obsidian's graph misses our real edges, its file list misses our titles, its sync fights
our append-only writes. The features that *would* be safe (read-only Canvas/graph as a desktop toy)
are the low-value ones. If this proceeds at all, it should be a **one-way, regenerated, read-only
projection vault** — never Obsidian as a co-writer of the live canonical files. Two-way "host"
is, on the evidence in `cc_board.py` and the live store, an inversion waiting to happen.
