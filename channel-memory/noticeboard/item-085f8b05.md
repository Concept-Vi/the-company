### 6. iOS app + Sync — HIGHEST VALUE TO TIM, HIGHEST FRICTION. Gated on infra.
This is the feature that most directly serves "everything from my phone." The Obsidian iOS app gives a native, offline, fast reading/searching surface over the vault, and Sync keeps it current. For a non-dev who lives on the phone, that's enormous.

**But it bites on a real, concrete gate:** our items live in **WSL** at `/home/tim/company/channel-memory/noticeboard/`. Per the canonical-vault-path memory, **WSL paths do not receive Obsidian mobile sync** — the synced vault is **Windows-side**. So mobile Obsidian over *these specific files* requires first solving *"get the noticeboard files into a Windows-side, Sync-enabled vault folder"* — a mirroring/bridging step that itself reopens the source-of-truth + conflict question (two writers, now across a filesystem boundary). High value, but **don't present it as free**; it's an infra project, not a toggle.
