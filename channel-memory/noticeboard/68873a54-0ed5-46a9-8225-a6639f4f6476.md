discover_capabilities_for_mode() was returning empty buckets for 4 of 7 affinity modes because no capabilities existed in those categories.

Root cause: affinity buckets (creative, diagnostic, learning, research) had no matching capability registry entries. Only 3 of 7 buckets (communication, content, coordination) had coverage.

Fix applied — 4 new capabilities registered (all L1, read-only):
- creative → creative.compose_content (executor: compose_block)
- diagnostic → diagnostic.system_health (executor: get_system_health)
- learning → learning.search_documentation (executor: search_documentation)
- research → research.full_text_search (executors: fts_search_content + search_resources)

All 7 affinity buckets now have at least one capability. Mode registry discovery is unblocked. Total capabilities: 43 (was 39).
