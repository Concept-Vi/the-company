On tablet viewport (768x1024), the Keeper chat panel is permanently expanded at the bottom and takes approximately 25% of the viewport. The Approve/Deny buttons on approval cards are partially obscured by the Keeper. At tablet width the Keeper should be collapsible or positioned as a side panel rather than a bottom-fixed overlay. Users need to see the full content of what they are approving without the chat covering it.
