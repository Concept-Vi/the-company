A comment marked "do this now" doesn't just sit there — it DISPATCHES the lead to actually do it, Tim watches it happen live, and the RESULT attaches back to his comment. The annotation becomes an instruction with its outcome threaded underneath. Only possible because the surface is wired into live sessions. Turns reviewing into directing. This is the capability a normal app cannot have.
