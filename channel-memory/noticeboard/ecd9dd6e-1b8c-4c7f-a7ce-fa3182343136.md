Every universal type defines component_spec with a Lucide icon, hex color, and renderer name. issue=bug/#ef4444, blocker=shield-alert/#ef4444, task=check-square/#3b82f6, milestone=flag/#8b5cf6, decision=scale/#f59e0b, design=palette/#06b6d4, proposal=lightbulb/#8b5cf6, request=file-text/#f59e0b, observation=search/#10b981, cognitive_guide=compass/#D4AF37, agent=bot/#6366f1, deployment=cloud/#06b6d4, model_provider=cpu/#8b5cf6, project_space=folder/#D4AF37, system_graph=git-branch/#6366f1, design_proposal=drafting-compass/#6366f1. Most also define behavior_spec with on_click:open_detail and on_create entrance fadeInUp animation. The front-end renders none of these — no icons, no colors, no type-specific cards, no behaviors. This is the single highest-impact activation: wire type icons and colors into every card, use type-specific renderers, and bind the behavior_spec actions.
