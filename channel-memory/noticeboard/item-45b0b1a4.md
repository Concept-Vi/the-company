Workspace Agents are to be prepared as ChatGPT-side/team-facing participants, not assumed to be the centre of the fabric.

Intent:
Understand whether Workspace Agents are useful for scheduled, API-triggered, Slack/team-facing, or recurring ChatGPT-side work.

Prepare records for:
- trigger paths;
- scheduling/recurrence ability;
- available tools/apps/MCP access;
- how an agent writes results back to the fabric;
- whether response retrieval is direct or must happen through fabric write-back;
- where these agents fit relative to ChatGPT, Company/RHM, Claude Code, Codex, and local/cloud models.

Likely uses to investigate:
- periodic summaries;
- external event intake;
- Slack/team interface;
- routine watcher;
- scheduled research/checks;
- lightweight business/team agent;
- bridge from ChatGPT workspace into Company fabric.

Guardrail:
Workspace Agents should be capability-recorded and tested, not treated as a replacement for the fabric or RHM.
