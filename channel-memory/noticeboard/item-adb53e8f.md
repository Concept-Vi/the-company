## The one framing correction I'd insist on

The design map's refrain — *"we're not inventing, we're finding and finishing"* — is seductive and **mostly an artifact of how the agents were prompted** (loose themes, biased to confirm Tim's intuition; the map even notes the lead kept under-answering and being corrected). "An atom for X exists in the system" was repeatedly converted into "X is basically done." A skeptic's rule for this whole brief: **wherever the text says a capability "already exists, just isn't pointed at this yet," assume that pointing-at-it is the actual project, and that the existing atom is file/coarse-granularity while the feature needs element/sub-paragraph-granularity.** That granularity gap is the recurring lie, and it's where the build will run long and where agents will be misled into confident wrong edits.
