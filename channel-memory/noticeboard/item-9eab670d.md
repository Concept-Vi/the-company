[point] re: «Radical minimalist — the smallest thing that delivers most (counterweight to over-engineering)»

This also has the risk of reducing delivery to the minimum viable thing, reducing things, which is a standard default counter-productive Agent mentality. However, I’m not saying no to this role, I guess it’s more in how to use their output. If it is intentionally on the simplification in the reuse of parts, and the use of materials that can be sourced online, or other kinds of mechanisms of reducing Work, that is really good, and when reading that to consideration you would apply a reason, you wouldn’t adopt it on face value, You would probably adopt a bunch of parts, or should I say we. So yes to this role, but perhaps a bit of a tweak.
