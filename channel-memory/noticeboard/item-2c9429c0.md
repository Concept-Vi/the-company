### 3.2 The `.obsidian/` config is another thing to own and sync (MEDIUM-LOW)
Adopting Obsidian means a `.obsidian/` dir (plugins, their settings, hotkeys, the Bases/Dataview
config, Front Matter Title config, theme). That is now:
- **State the build must manage and version** (or it's not reproducible across desktop/mobile).
- A **per-device divergence** source (mobile disables some plugins; community plugins are desktop-only
  by default — so the readable-title trick that works on desktop may **not work on the phone**, the
  one place it's needed).
- Another sync surface that can conflict.
It's not catastrophic, but it's real ongoing overhead for a benefit that §2.1/§2.2 show is thin.
