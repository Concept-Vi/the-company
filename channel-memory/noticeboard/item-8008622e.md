## 8 · Threads worth pulling (not decisions — for Tim to feel out)

- **C is more "us" than it looks.** "You annotate, you don't overwrite" IS mark-is-truth. An inbox-replay
  intake plane (C) where iOS input lands as *new* observations folds perfectly into the append-only graph
  — it may be the most architecturally honest of the four, not a compromise.
- **The projection is itself a lens-renderer the company already wants.** Emitting a vault is the same act
  as rendering the phone surface or the gallery — one canonical store, many regenerated faces. Obsidian
  becomes "just another face," which is exactly the company's own design grain (one truth, many lenses).
- **`.base` files as saved company views.** Dataview/Bases over the projected frontmatter (`type`,
  `state`, `channel`, `source`) reproduce `list_items`' projections as live tables — for free, as a read
  lens, with zero risk to truth.
- **The §4 YAML check is the single highest-value experiment** before any code: it eliminates or unlocks
  a whole option in one test on one copied file.
