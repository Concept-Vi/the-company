↻ directives from capabilities().mode_directives — registry is truth
◉listening
Conversational and present; responds fully (voice in/out).
⌨text-only
Responds in text, concisely, only to what is addressed.
◌background
Minimal — surfaces only what genuinely needs you; otherwise a one-line ack.
◎focus
You are in deep work — extremely brief (a line or two), no elaboration unless asked.
➤walkthrough
Actively guides — narrates each step and directs your attention.
👁watch-and-react
Observes over your shoulder; comments only when relevant, briefly.
⚡decide-for-me
Acts on the reversible / AUTO classes itself (deterministic by consequence); still cannot self-approve — anything needing approval is surfaced.
