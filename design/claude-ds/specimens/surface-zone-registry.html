Surface-Zone Registry

Near-white surfaces mark containment depth (zoning = depth; see core/containers.css). These ten semantic tones are the opt-in layer on top — each a pigment mixed toward the ground at a low percentage, scaled by one --zone-intensity knob. Warm throughout. The differences should be felt more than seen.

The gold rule — gold is the only saturated voice, reserved for Vi, active decisions, selected states, key emphasis and primary actions. Never a default background.
