Shapes carry typed meaning — a vessel is not decoration, it tells you what kind of thing sits inside. The system spans the regular polygons from 3 to 8 sides (never more) plus the circle (the limit of ∞ sides). An icon from the icon system sits inside each shape, and every part of the treatment — edge colour, fill sheen, shadow, thickness, ink — resolves from design tokens through one default that applies system-wide. Everything is a parameter: the cascade is markDefaults (tokens) ◀ shape-type ◀ markSVG(opts), so a single call can retint, restroke or reshape any vessel without leaving the system.
