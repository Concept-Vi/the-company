CV_HOST — the environment layer

The bridge between Vi and the repository, built in the same shape as CV_AI: a registry of pluggable runtimes resolved by capability, a serializer that turns any registry mutation into exact source for its one home file, and a reviewable change queue. capability-available = f(environment, runtime). Sandbox stages for review; a connected runtime writes to disk. This card is a live projection of CV_HOST.describe().
