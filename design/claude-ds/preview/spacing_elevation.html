Elevation

Nine tiers, layered to feel like paper on paper. Every shadow stacks a hairline contact, a warm bronze ambient, and a quiet inset top-light so the surface reads as material — not floating, not glued. Never cool, never harsh.

00 Resting y 0 Flush surface. Hairline highlight only — for sections and panels that read as the page itself.
01 xs y 1 · 2 List rows, dividers, the lightest pressable. Just enough to suggest a seam.
02 sm y 1 · 3 Chips, badges, secondary buttons at rest. The "leaf on paper" tier.
03 md ★ y 1 · 6 · 14 The default card. Most surfaces live here — dashboards, content cards, panels.
04 lg y 2 · 10 · 22 Hover & selected states. The lift you feel before you see the cursor land.
05 xl y 3 · 16 · 34 Featured tiles, hero cards, marketing surfaces. Generous depth, still quiet.
06 pop y 2 · 8 · 18 Floating popovers, menus, chat bubbles, the gold comment marker.
07 modal y 4 · 24 · 48 Dialogs, drawers, sheets. The room behind the page — softly cast, never theatrical.
08 gold glow y 6 · 16 Reserved for the primary action and active brand tokens — a warm halo, never a hard ring.
Color recipe Contact shadow rgba(31,26,18, .04) over a bronze ambient rgba(152,128,88, .10). Cool greys are forbidden.
Inset top-light Every elevated surface carries inset 0 1px 0 rgba(255,253,245,.85) — the hairline of paper that makes shadow feel earned.
Discipline Step one tier at a time on hover. Never combine glow with modal. Never stack md on md — escalate to lg.
