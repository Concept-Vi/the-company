The staged-process pattern reads as a vertical ladder: flat white cards with a logo-gold border and centered gold-brown titles, joined by solid gold connectors (endpoint dot · line · ▼). The between-stage gap is a pill on the connector — 1 – 2 Weeks / Revision — and a ⊕ join appends the deliverable. Each stage taps out to the deliverable image at that stage, where render fidelity escalates from grey massing to a hero render. (Vessels reuse the container + connector system; titles use --accent-gold-deep.)
