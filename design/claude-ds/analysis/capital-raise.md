# capital-raise — folder analysis (30pp, 3:2 dense, 1893×1287)

> The one corpus folder never analysed (PROGRESS had it ☐). It is a **capital-raise pitch** — the
> deepest-LOD, longest member of the pitch family (30pp incl. a photographic Appendix). It both
> **re-confirms** the model on a 3rd+ folder AND surfaces several pieces of grammar not yet written
> down. Content translated to generic roles per the GUIDE (content-agnostic). Newest, richest
> single source after `pitch-deck` and `recent-pitches`.

## TL;DR — what this folder adds
1. **Running-sentence titles** (NEW, strong) — the deck's slide titles are *one grammatical sentence*
   spread across slides, each beginning with a connective (But / To Support / With / That / Which /
   By Leveraging / And / Because). The narrative thread lives in the **title chain**, not just body.
2. **Product-surface bookends** (NEW) — cover (p1) and back cover (p30) are the *live product*
   (panotour viewer chrome: MENU, info-hotspot `i`, account glyph, circular hotspot markers) with a
   **frosted-glass overlay panel** (title lockup / contact card). The brand frame IS the product.
3. **Full-bleed photographic section divider** (p20 "Appendix") — single word, muted bronze/sage,
   top-right; no chrome. Distinct from the cover.
4. **Clean-white ground variant** — content slides sample **#ffffff** (not the warm ivory `#fdfcf7`
   of pitch-deck/deck1-2026). A *theme sub-mode* (light·clean) on the same ladder, not a contradiction.
5. **Sage-green semantic accent** (~`#9fbc73`) — the p5 legend literally labels green **"Communication"**
   (vs gold "File Upload & Update"); recurs as green `$`/relationship connectors (p10) and the
   sustainability mark (p28). A *second semantic hue* beyond the gold ramp. (Needs a 2nd folder to graduate.)
6. **Five diagram sub-types not yet in DIAGRAMS.md**: orbital verb-ring, stacked/expandable pipeline
   node, 1-D spectrum axis, manifold/converging-summation, progressive-fidelity vertical stepper.
7. **Ramp-bordered card row** — a row of outline cards whose **stroke colour steps along the
   gold→bronze ramp** (encodes sequence/heat), the cards overlapping the zone band beneath them (p18).

## Level 1 — Surface & format
- **Ratio 3:2** (1893×1287), same as `recent-pitches`. Confirms **ratio ⟂ density/LOD** again.
- **Two surface registers inside one artifact:** *full-bleed image* (cover p1, divider p20, back p30)
  vs *white analytical band* (everything else). The blueprint-ghost texture + diagonal-hatch strips
  (top + bottom band edges, and as stat-strip frames) ride the white band; the image surfaces carry
  none of it (the photo is the texture). Bottom-right **V monogram** watermark on every analytical slide.
- **Ground = #ffffff** (spread 0–2 = trustworthy flat). The ivory decks and this white deck are the
  same `light` theme at two **clean↔warm** sub-positions — a knob, not a redraw.

## Level 2 — Grid, margins, alignment
- **~12% side margins** hold (title baseline + body left edge ≈ 0.06–0.12 of frame), same as the 16:9
  and other 3:2 decks → margin ratio is **ratio-invariant**, re-confirmed a 4th time.
- **Title:** centered, bold, near-black ink, ~7% from top, balanced (2-line wraps kept symmetric).
  This deck centers titles (vs left-set in some); centered-title is the dense-pitch dialect.
- **Lede:** full-measure paragraph directly under title, ink-grey, ~2 lines, then the slide body.
- Strong **two-column** (content-left / diagram-right) and **2×2 panel** discipline; everything snaps
  to a shared baseline grid. Bullets hang-indent with the glyph in the margin.

## Level 3 — Zoning & depth (sampled)
- Zone washes here are **panels on white**: soft rounded `--zone` cards (`#f7f6f1`-ish warm-neutral),
  hatch-framed stat strips, and outline-only panels (gold stroke, no fill — p27). The ~2% undertone
  ladder holds; on a white ground the depth reads through the **rounded soft-shadow panels** rather
  than nested washes. Confirms zoning = depth, expressed via panel elevation when the ground is white.
- **Cards overlap zones** (p18): four reason-cards sit *on top of* the two summary zone-bands,
  bridging two depth levels — an intentional z-stack join.

## Level 9 — Narrative architecture (the deepest layer)
- **The title chain is a single sentence.** Reading titles in order: *…Poor Design Coordination
  Creates Billions… → We're Bringing the Third Epoch… → By Leveraging Product-Led Growth… → With a
  Single System… → Which We've Tested in Multiple Verticals → But our Growth Stage is Strategically
  Focused → To Support a Staggered SaaS Release → Our Go-to-Market is Organic… → With a Clear Value
  Proposition → That We've Market Tested and Sold → Which Has Generated a Diverse Pipeline → That
  We're Leagues in Front of → And Our Long Term Plan is Already Underway → …Because We Know How to
  Spend it.* The **leading connective** (But/To/With/That/Which/By/And/Because) makes each title a
  *clause continuing the prior* — the argument is legible from the title rail alone. This is the
  single strongest narrative rule the corpus has surfaced. Body slides then *prove* the clause.
- **Arc**: problem (cost of disconnection) → thesis (3rd epoch / connect) → mechanism (single system,
  PLG) → proof (verticals, value prop, traction, pipeline) → moat (competitive) → ask (why now, deal,
  use of funds) → appendix (market, product depth, case studies, team, contact).
- **LOD**: this is the *long/high-detail* rung (multi-section slides, full prose ledes, an Appendix).
  Same skeleton as the terse decks at lower LOD → re-confirms **LOD as an axis orthogonal to surface**.
- **Restraint holds**: no gradients-as-decoration, no emoji, sentence-case body, gold used sparingly
  for *activation* only, one structural bronze, green only for the communication semantic.
