The container universal component

Blocks — the tangible units of zones

The Glyphic proved the grammar for marks: parts · slots · sockets, every value resolved from an axis. The Block is the same grammar for containers — what glyphics, typed text, and every primitive live inside. A block has a ground (the plane behind it, texture from the full texture axis), a material surface (glass, velum, parchment, stone — or none), a coordinate in depth (the level axis: the containment ladder, which derives the depth treatment and the spacing rhythm), ordered sockets holding typed occupants, and action sockets (verbs from the actions registry). Whole pages are compositions of blocks; every surface and every word below is produced by resolutions of the one operation.
