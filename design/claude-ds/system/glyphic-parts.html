A glyphic is its parts

Under one unit sit three parts: the outer ring (the n-gon vessel), the fill plane it bounds, and the symbol on top. Each part owns its colour, texture and motion — cleanly separate — while the whole still scales, elevates and moves as one. The mark is drawn by a layered compositor (one markSVG layer per part, stacked) and edited by the one inspector, which shows a section per part. Everything below is live from CV_GLYPHIC.compose and CV_INSPECTOR.
