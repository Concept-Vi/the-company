# The Company — system folder

This folder holds the **Company's own context**: the principles it operates under, the architecture it grows along, the open threads it knows are unfinished. It is *context-code* — instructions for the AI side of a hybrid AI/software system, sitting alongside the eventual deterministic code. The Company is a typed compositional dataflow system; this folder is part of the substrate it runs on.

## Who reads this

The primary readers are future agents working inside the Company. **No human routinely reads, writes, reviews, or edits these files.** That fact conditions everything: what gets written here is the concrete record that shapes every later agent, and the system cannot lean on a human catching drift. The doctrine here must be self-maintaining under thousands of equal agents.

Tim reviews and steers when something is off; he is the origin the system resolves against and the final adjudicator of direction. He does not consume the synthesis like a reader; he corrects it like a chief.

## How it is laid out

- **`principles.md`** — what the Company knows about itself, expressed as understanding. Read this first.
- **`architecture.md`** — the structural shape: compositional dataflow, addresses, the interface, the escalation ladder, the genetic kernel, the open design threads.
- **`../exchanges/`** — the verbatim source: Tim's actual messages and what each produced. The history the synthesis here is derived from. When in doubt, go to the source.
- **`../TIM.md`** — a parallel foundation document, written in a separate branch and covering overlapping ground. Neither it nor this folder is canonical; Tim hasn't decided how to reconcile them. Ask him if a question or conflict comes up.

## How it is maintained

- **One coherent body, maintained by integration.** New understanding does not stack as dated additions; it integrates into the existing text, with every related place updated to keep the whole cohesive. Drift, bloat, and slow degradation of influence on future agents are what append-only produces under thousands of equal writers.
- **Timeless, authorless, present-tense.** No "as of date X" stamps, no "Layer N" strata, no revision logs inside the synthesis. History lives in the exchanges archive — the immutable source layer — where time legitimately belongs. The synthesis is *always now*, derived from the growing log. (This split — immutable log + always-current derived projection — is **CQRS / event-sourcing** in software terms.)
- **Provisional and open.** Sections that are gestured-at but not designed are marked. Every list ends with the marker *examples; more exist*. The system knows what it doesn't yet know.
- **Understanding, not instructions.** Future agents must not be able to obey this folder; they must be able to understand from it and decide. Specifics and prescriptive directives rot; principles and understanding generalise.

## Open

This folder is a seed, not a finished structure. It captures what has surfaced through the founding exchanges. Splits, new files, and reorganisations will happen as more is understood. The structure itself is provisional.

Specific open threads:
- The mechanism by which integration is enforced (coherence-linter; constitution-as-machine-rules) is named but not built.
- The escalation ladder's middle rung (the model of Tim — the twin) is named but not built.
- The folder's own evolution norms (when to split a file, how to merge, how to handle conflict between agents writing here) are gestured at, not designed.
