# universal-mechanics

## What it is
Tim's upstream **science/framework vault** — the conceptual bedrock the Company is one instantiation of. An Obsidian corpus (89M, ~6355 files) articulating a single original theory: the **"substrate-environment"**, the universal structural form that *any* system operating over addressable, structured content necessarily presupposes, regardless of domain. It is explicitly NOT a build spec — it is the *form* that specific builds (the Company among them) instantiate.

> "The substrate-environment is the *structural form within which any operation over substrate-articulated content operates*… it is not a specific system, application, or platform; it is the structural form any such system instantiates." — `/home/tim/universal-mechanics/universal-substrate-system/the-substrate-environment.md` §1

The theory has three faces: a **canonical theorem** ("Function of Relative Definition", in `working-docs/universal-mechanics-main/`), a **mathematical architecture** ("ConceptV"), and an **operational/verification layer** (`math-verification/`, with real proofs and a vi-invariant library). The vault is self-referential — it operates the mechanics it describes (typed notes, addresses, registries, status-chains).

## Key ideas worth mining
This is the deepest idea-source in the resource set. The primitives, concretely:
- **The eight substrate-mechanics** (the spine, `the-substrate-environment.md` §2/§12): `substrate-store` (content-addressable holding), `type-registry` (types as typed entities with cascading rules), `relation-layer` (typed connections / slots-sockets), `operation-registry` (typed operations with pre/postconditions), `multi-axis-index`, `projection-layer` (schemas as reading-lenses), `interaction-layer` ("interaction is what activates a thing"), `verification-layer` (structural-property comparison, no semantic interpretation).
- **Axes-and-orientations** (`axes-and-orientations.md`) — the literal upstream of invariant-resolution. An axis = a *functor* F: Substrate → Coord_F projecting the substrate into one coordinate space. Catalogued axes: structural, graph, semantic, type, temporal, content-/structural-fingerprint, authority/source, articulation-state, confidence/certainty, domain, articulation-vector. *"The same entity lives in every axis simultaneously"*; the catalogue is **open** — new axes are new functors, the substrate never changes.
- **Multi-orientation pullback as the universal query form** — view / search / compare are the *same* operation (a wide pullback over N axis-legs): AND=limit, OR=colimit, difference=equaliser, ranking=reciprocal-rank-fusion.
- **The implication-graph** — any definition *implicates* what else must exist ("an application implicates a UI implicates interaction-elements"); implications are first-class queryable entities.
- **Cross-scale invariance / operational fractality** — the same mechanics operate at every containment level; map-reduce as a substrate-mechanic, not an algorithm.
- **Self-containment / bootstrap** — registries contain their own meta-types; the system extends itself through use, no external supervisor.
- **Dispatch-and-amplification** — content arrives → matched to types → operations dispatch → outputs become new entities; iterated, structure accumulates. Tim is the trajectory-navigator-via-information-introduction, not a per-output verifier.
- **Identity-as-trajectory; time-as-coordinate** — bi-temporal (transaction-time + valid-time), so replay / time-travel / counterfactuals are structurally automatic.
- **Verification-as-arithmetic** (`math-verification/README.md`): *"epistemological problems (did the agent do the work?) can be reduced to arithmetic ones."* The deep boundary is **Rice's theorem** — completeness is decidable, quality is not.
- **No-developer principle** — the engine is modifiable ONLY through substrate-mediated operations, never external developer intervention; fail loud. Direct ancestor of the Company's instrument-authors / everything-is-a-variable / git-revert-is-the-safety rules.
- **Authorship-asymmetry** — Tim = source-of-substrate-content, AI = articulation-amplifier; co-authorship structurally impossible.
- **Cognitive-entanglement** — coupled human-AI document traversal (human reads aloud, AI captures reactive commentary as structured extensions); observation changes both; autopoietic.
- **Candidacy / six-states-of-unity** — lifecycle register: nothing asserted as settled, everything is candidacy at proto-state (ancestor of always-more-to-the-theorem / scaffolding-not-spec).
