## The cross-cutting insights (the keystones this round produced)

1. **Rule-vs-role is THE classifier (E3).** Every combine/aggregate/route pattern decomposes by one test: deterministic over resolved values → a **rule** (predicate grammar); needs model judgment → a **role** the rules wire. This makes *all* of MoA / blackboard / quorum / debate L2-legal by construction, and tells you where model-fusion lives (a role, quarantined off the spine). → fold into G3.
2. **The applications ARE the reshape findings (E1).** The four post-voice targets aren't copies of the voice stream — they're **five thought-shape archetypes**: `linear-stream` (voice) · `reduce-tree` (codebase map-reduce) · `jury-select` (altitude-translation) · `scatter-route` (typed-triage) · `scatter-write` (background). Each forces exactly one of R1–R4 (jury · typed-lane sink · activation budget · join/fanout) — so building the four IS building the reshapes. `THOUGHT_SHAPES` = these ~5 archetypes, built once. Order: K → M → B → I. The organ: M = afferent nerve · B = heartbeat (idle-VRAM, feeds the others) · K = deep read · I = taste.
3. **Rendering is the keystone of GOVERNANCE, not polish (E2+E4).** Propose-not-apply safety depends entirely on Tim being able to *perceive what he approves*. The cognition-view and the safety story are the same thing. → elevates G7 from "nice" to load-bearing.
4. **Mode is a {mode × activation-context} matrix on a KV-seesaw (E5).** Mode dials depth-of-context ⇄ breadth-of-concurrency on the one card; "direct" modes still fire a small inject-cast (one part, not zero roles); a new mode is ~recombination of ~6 shared roles. → enriches G5.
5. **Epistemic monoculture is the sharpest blind-spot (E4).** 32 roles on one 4B are 32 *correlated* samples; a jury that varies only seed/temp measures variance, not independent error → false confidence. A correctness-jury that matters needs **model diversity** (the E3 0.8B second model / a cloud tiebreak). → a real constraint on how juries are trusted.
6. **The self-tuning loop is the biggest unnamed opportunity (E4).** Close the introspective rollup loop not just onto telemetry but onto the **registries that define the thinking** — retire misfiring rules, promote recurring resolutions to faculties (automaticity), reweight casts. Nearly free (L1 + the introspective law + propose-not-apply). Also the deepest drift risk. → a future phase + a governance concern.
