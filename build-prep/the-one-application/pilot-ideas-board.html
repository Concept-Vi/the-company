Open — to think about together

The pilot interaction

  • How does choosing its tool call feel? The palette appears, you pick — then what?
  • How does it "ask you things as it goes" — inline, a prompt, a fork in the path?
  • How do you redirect mid-flight without breaking its thread?

Space & movement

  • What ARE the coordinates — meaning-distance? the address space? both?
  • How does perspective-switching work — a gesture, a key, a dial?
  • What does "flying" look like — a path through points, a camera move?

The cognition under it

  • How do the small models' coverage feed what it proposes?
  • When does the panel/jury show its verdicts to you?
  • Where do you sit as the judge — above the auto-judge, or instead of it?
