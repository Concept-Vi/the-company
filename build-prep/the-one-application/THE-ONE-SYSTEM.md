## LEGIBILITY ARCHITECTURE (Tim ratified, 2026-06-17 #3) — meaning lives in the registries, not the surface
The universal-projection instrument is **all variables**: aim it at any address/registry and dial what centre/angle/radius/poles MEAN. The events corpus was just the FIRST thing loaded, not the subject. **Consequence: nothing that carries meaning may live IN the instrument — all meaning comes FROM the data.**

So the human MEANING of every addressable thing becomes **self-describing FIELDS in the registries** (or linked from them): a human name · what-it-is · what-fills-it · why-you'd-look. As **DECLARED REQUIREMENTS** — born-filled when a type is created, backfilled once for existing things. The instrument/gallery just READS those fields for whatever it's aimed at and renders them, staying empty of meaning itself.

★ This is the **architecture for the standing law "translate everything → human meaning"** (Tim never sees machine names; the surface shows what-is-it / what-data / why) — now realized as DATA, not ad-hoc surface-logic. It is the FACTORY/registry layer doing its job: the gallery renders THROUGH the registries' self-describing fields. **Owner: composition** (registry field-schemas + the declared-requirement + backfill mechanism — build ON what it holds, reflect-first). The WHICH-fields design is a pedagogy/drill-down/viewer-journey question (first-entry → see → do → might-want → branches), NOT a quick field-list. Engine (projection's G10/G9) stays at-bar; it's the renderer of these fields, empty of meaning itself.
