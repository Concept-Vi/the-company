The unification, in one line

The organs already exist. The collective is: one address space (everything typed) · small models cover it (breadth) · the RHM reasons + proposes (depth) · you pilot from the projected palette (judgment + direction) · one projected surface makes all of it a single space you fly through. The RHM is the engine and the commander; the dials are pilot⇄autopilot; the modes are views and postures over the one thing.
