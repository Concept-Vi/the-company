### 2.2 — THE INVARIANT LAW: what every axis obeys at every scale

The deepest lens. The law re-grounds as a small family of statements that are all **one relationship that
survives total content-substitution**. Full categorical grounding in [[WIDEN-FINDINGS]] LENS 3.

- **The cascade = a categorical morphism.** An address is the unique arrow from the root to a point;
  **decontextualisation is structurally impossible because a thing without its ancestors is not-an-arrow** (a
  type-error, not merely missing metadata). Addressing composes the arrow *up*; resolution traverses it *down*
  — two inverse readings of one morphism. (Law-level justification for fail-loud on un-rooted things.)
- **Visibility = legality** — the same claim, read in the type-face. What is visible is what is legal from the
  collective type system; an unregistered type has no object to be an arrow *from*, so it cannot be referenced,
  so it is invisible. The closed-world reading (unregistered → does not exist) is **derived** from the morphism
  law, not assumed alongside it.
- **Fractal operationally, not geometrically.** Same operations at every scale; the contents/vocabulary vary.
  You cannot tell which scale you are at by the operations — only by the content. (This is the §1 spine's
  point-3, read from the law side, and it absorbs the S2 "scale" seam: scale *is* this shared recursion.)
- **The unit/edge duality — name ⊥ extension.** At every referent there is a unit (a file) and an edge over
  units (a folder); a **continuous slot (the name — any legal string, open/free)** and a **discrete slot (the
  extension/type — the frame's registered vocabulary, closed)**. These are the resolver's two limbs:
  **derive** (continuous) | **select** (discrete). ★ Tim's 06-19 transmission **fills a real gap here**: the
  formal body had the discrete/type half and the slot/socket pair, but the **continuous-name half was absent**,
  and the **folder-defined-by-its-distinct-absence-of-an-extension** weld is nowhere in the canon. This is his
  net-new connection of the unit/edge duality to the absence law.
- **Absence = declared ⊖ occupancy.** You read a hole by its rim: a slot whose address is declared but whose
  content is empty is a legible **distinct-absence** (the open seam, the skeleton, the folder). Absence is
  evaluated *against the registry, never against the void*. Only the declared-but-empty case is computable —
  which is why filtering surfaces declared absences and clustering surfaces undeclared-but-pressured ones
  (gap-pressure).
- **★ KEYSTONE — "everything is a variable" is the META-law.** Each law above is *a relationship that holds
  while its content ranges over everything*: the cascade over variable coordinates, authority over variable
  vocabularies, fractality over variable schemas, name⊥extension over variable strings, absence over variable
