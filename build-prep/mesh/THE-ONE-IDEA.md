## 0. The compression

extracted once, laid over the address tree, run at every scale, with meaning itself given the
proposal lifecycle — so that remembering stops being an act and becomes a property of the world.**

## 1. The law that was already everywhere
Every working subsystem is already a two-layer pyramid: cheap total coverage below, expensive
judgment above, an explicit resolution boundary between.
- recall: pplx bi-encoder (cheap, total, isolated vectors) → jina reranker (reads pair together,
  applied only to the surfaced top). The pool-cap-12 bug was a *break in the boundary* — the
  judgment layer starved by an obsolete economic assumption. Pyramid pathology lives at boundaries.
- dragnet: neutral coarse (D3 — no relevance judgment allowed) → step-gate → fine.
- transcript miner: mine_exchange fans cheap → judge_mining validates a sample, biased to fail.
- mesh round 1: observers fan → one triangulator reasons across.
- juries: verify_lens fans lenses → deterministic tally; the E4 caveat ("N draws on ONE model are
  correlated") is the pyramid asking for its missing lower layer.
- the proposal lifecycle itself: agents propose in volume; Tim's gate judges.
The dragnet/miner/recall/drift-radar feel like siblings that never met because each independently
evolved the same shape; it was never extracted into a shared spine. Mesh round-1's own diagnosis:
"not missing a single component; missing COMPLETION" — the components are pyramid fragments.

## 3. Memory is the same pyramid on its side
Layers of resolution, each an INDEX into the layer beneath, no layer ever destroying the one below:
LAWS (operator rules, confirmed) ▲ PATTERNS (clusters, canonical tags, guides) ▲ EXTRACTS
(design_intent · history · dragnet-fine) ▲ TAGS (0.8B/4B + deterministic — the missing layers) ▲
RAW (exchange:// transcripts · code:// files — verbatim, the sole record).
Compression without loss of provenance: every layer points down via addresses. The recollection
lineage's mined law — "raw and distilled are not either/or; raw carries decision-rationale that
distillation destroys" — is promoted from a fork's design choice to a LAW of the memory pyramid.
The memory hierarchy and the cognitive hierarchy are the SAME hierarchy: each memory layer is
maintained by the model tier whose economics match its volume. recall (@recall) stays the query
face; recollection's body retires, its law survives. **Standing practice: extract the law before
retiring any dormant pocket** — retirement without law-extraction is how knowledge died.

## 4. The address tree is the nervous system; causation runs both ways
- Aggregation upward: a directory KNOWS its children's composition; the repo root carries a live

  current state" becomes GENERATED, not maintained; orienteering becomes the human face of

- exchange:// ↔ code:// are the two halves of a CAUSAL record (conversation caused everything; no

- The pre-formed tree already practices "the thing declares what it is" (ext=type, name=id); the
  nucleated taxonomy is the tree's type column growing new refinement levels — one system, not two.

## 5. Dormancy becomes a standing query
dormant = old + no consumer-edges + no recent touch (a WHERE clause, not an expedition).
parked vs forgotten becomes MECHANICAL via the no-unconditional-deferrals law: parked carries its
condition-address (return-when) in the tree; forgotten doesn't. floor_walk/drift_radar promote from
expeditions to standing views. The disease was that remembering was an act.

## 6. The economics inversion
Every sampling decision (11 curated sessions, 8 territories, pool caps, draw counts) was a place a
planner pre-decided what mattered — the exact thing triangulation-not-planning rejects, previously
economically forced. Warm local pyramid ⇒ coverage unrationed ⇒ "what should we look at?" (planning)
dies, replaced by "everything is looked at; what needs judgment?" (routing). Attention allocation
becomes an emergent property of aggregated state. Plus: error-correction through volume — one
careful judgment is fragile; ten thousand careless observations clustered are robust. The pyramid
converts cheap unreliability into aggregate reliability (evolution's/markets'/perception's trick) —
the thing a single-context agent structurally cannot do alone. Complementarity: Fable can't be a
volume instrument; the swarm can't hold the whole picture.

## A1 — The models run INSIDE the universe (MCP is a door, not the residence)
Tim: "it is intended that the company models will run inside the universe, as opposed to through
MCP. MCP will still be a main access, but they will also be inside the universe and by that I mean
not using traditional chat session storage, but **dynamic resolution of context from variables,
with data driven triggers and actions, so the whole universe is one session traced back to its
route** [root]."
Implication for the pyramid: the layers are not batch jobs run AT the estate from outside — they
are resident processes OF the universe. A model's context is not a chat transcript; it is RESOLVED
AT FIRE TIME from addressed variables (the input-address mechanism — run://, cas://, context:// —
already prototypes this). Triggers are DATA (state changes on tree nodes fire roles — the
rules/trigger machinery in the role registry is the seed). The "one session traced back to its
root" = the universe's whole history IS the session; an agent turn is a point in it, its context a
dynamic resolution over the tree, not a stored scrollback. The heartbeat (build step 6) is the
first native inhabitant: data-driven triggers (what changed) resolving context from variables (the
addressed state) firing actions (re-observation). Design the layers from day one so MCP-driving is
one face and in-universe residence is the destination.

## A2 — There is even more dormant, and coverage is no longer the constraint
Tim: "there is even more to find, even more related mechanisms that want to be part of it, that are
the shapes, dormant... coverage and breadth doesn't seem so much of a problem anymore."
Implication: §1's five instances are a FLOOR, not an inventory. The dormancy dragnet (§5) and the
total-coverage sieve must run over EVERYTHING — every transcript, every module, every registry —
expecting to find more pyramid fragments and more mechanisms "that want to be part of it." The
